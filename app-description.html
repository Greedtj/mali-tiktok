App Description

Last updated: May 27, 2026

Mali Studio Creator Automation is a small creator workflow tool for preparing short, vertical lifestyle videos and publishing approved videos to the creator's connected TikTok account. The intended content is daily slow-life vlog material such as scenic drives, cafe visits, travel moments, and simple everyday clips. The app is operated by the account owner and is not a public social network, scraping tool, analytics marketplace, or mass-posting service.

How the app works

  1. The creator selects photos or short video snippets that they own or have permission to use.
  2. The app assembles a 5-10 second vertical video draft with a simple caption and light visual treatment.
  3. The creator reviews the draft and chooses whether it should be published.
  4. After the creator authorizes the app through TikTok OAuth, the app publishes only the approved video.
  5. The app displays basic upload status so the creator can confirm whether the video was published.

Requested scopes and data use

Scope Purpose Data obtained Use in the app
user.info.basic Confirm the connected creator account. Basic profile details such as username, display name, avatar, and account identifier. Shown in the dashboard so the creator knows which account is connected before publishing.
video.publish Publish creator-approved short videos. Video file, caption, and publishing parameters submitted by the app. Used only to upload a video after the creator has prepared and approved it inside the app.
video.list Check recent publish status and avoid duplicate posting. Video IDs, publish status, timestamps, and basic video metadata for the connected account. Displayed as recent upload history and used to confirm whether an app-generated clip was posted.
public.user.info Personalize the creator dashboard. Public profile information made available by the platform. Used to label the connected account and improve the account-specific creator workflow.

Data protection and limits

The app collects only the data needed to identify the connected account, create the selected video draft, publish approved content, and show upload status. It does not access private messages, does not scrape content, does not sell user data, and does not share user data with advertising networks.

Suggested review submission reason

We are requesting access so Mali Studio Creator Automation can let a creator connect their TikTok account, generate short 5-10 second slow-life vlog videos from their own media, review the generated draft, and publish the approved video to their own account. The requested scopes are used only for account identification, publishing approved videos, displaying recent upload status, and personalizing the creator dashboard.
