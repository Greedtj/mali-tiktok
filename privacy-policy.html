Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

Mali Studio Creator Automation helps a creator prepare short 5-10 second vertical lifestyle videos and publish approved videos to the creator's connected TikTok account. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used.

Information we collect

  • Basic account information from the connected TikTok account, such as username, display name, avatar, public profile information, and account identifier.
  • OAuth access tokens required to perform authorized actions after the creator grants permission.
  • Creator-selected media, generated video metadata, captions, scheduling preferences, and upload status.
  • Basic operational logs for security, troubleshooting, duplicate-post prevention, and service reliability.

How we use information

  • To show which creator account is connected before any publishing action occurs.
  • To create short video drafts from media selected by the creator.
  • To publish only videos that the creator approves for upload.
  • To display recent upload history, publishing status, and error messages.
  • To maintain security, detect misuse, and improve app reliability.

Platform scopes

Scope Data accessed Use
user.info.basic Basic profile and account identity. Identify and display the connected creator account.
video.publish Approved video file, caption, and publish request. Publish creator-approved videos to the connected account.
video.list Recent video IDs, status, timestamps, and basic metadata. Show upload status and avoid duplicate posting.
public.user.info Public profile information. Personalize account labels in the dashboard.

Sharing and sale of data

We do not sell personal data. We do not share personal data with advertising networks. Data is sent to TikTok only when required to authenticate the connected account, publish an approved video, or check publishing status through TikTok's official developer platform.

Retention and deletion

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the creator workflow, support troubleshooting, protect the service, or comply with applicable obligations. A creator may request deletion at any time by emailing hello@malistudio.dev.

Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact Mali Studio at hello@malistudio.dev.
