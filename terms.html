Purpose

Mali TikTok Studio is used to create short slow-life vlog videos and publish approved videos to a connected TikTok account through TikTok's official developer platform.

User responsibilities

  • You must own or have permission to use all media, music, captions, and other content submitted to the app.
  • You must not upload content that is illegal, harmful, misleading, infringing, or violates TikTok policies.
  • You are responsible for reviewing generated videos before publishing.
  • You may disconnect TikTok access at any time from your TikTok account settings.

Automation limits

The app is designed for lightweight creator workflow automation. It is not intended for spam, mass posting, platform manipulation, scraping, or unauthorized access.
