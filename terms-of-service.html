Service purpose

Mali Studio Creator Automation is a creator-operated workflow tool for preparing short vertical lifestyle videos and publishing creator-approved videos to a connected TikTok account. The service is intended for personal or small creator use, including slow-life vlog clips, scenic drives, cafe moments, travel clips, and similar daily life content.
