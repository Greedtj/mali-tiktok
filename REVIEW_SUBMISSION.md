# TikTok Review Resubmission Text

## App Description

Mali Studio Creator Automation is a creator workflow tool that helps the account owner prepare short 5-10 second vertical lifestyle videos and publish approved videos to their connected TikTok account. The intended content is daily slow-life vlog material, such as scenic drives, cafe visits, travel moments, and simple everyday clips. The app is operated by the creator and is not a public social network, scraping tool, analytics marketplace, or mass-posting service.

The creator selects photos or short video snippets that they own or have permission to use. The app assembles a short vertical video draft with a simple caption and light visual treatment. The creator reviews the draft and chooses whether it should be published. After the creator authorizes the app through TikTok OAuth, the app publishes only the approved video and displays basic upload status.

## Review Description / Submission Reason

We are requesting access so Mali Studio Creator Automation can let a creator connect their TikTok account, generate short 5-10 second slow-life vlog videos from their own media, review the generated draft, and publish the approved video to their own account.

Requested scopes and use:

- `user.info.basic`: Used to identify and display the connected TikTok account in the dashboard before publishing. Data used includes basic profile details such as username, display name, avatar, and account identifier.
- `video.publish`: Used to publish only creator-approved short videos. Data used includes the video file, caption, and publishing parameters submitted by the app.
- `video.list`: Used to check recent publish status and avoid duplicate posting. Data used includes video IDs, publish status, timestamps, and basic video metadata for the connected account.
- `public.user.info`: Used to personalize the creator dashboard with public profile information and account labels.

The app does not access private messages, does not scrape content, does not sell user data, and does not share user data with advertising networks. It uses platform data only for account identification, approved video publishing, recent upload status, and account-specific creator workflow features.
