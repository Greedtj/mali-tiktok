Data Deletion

Last updated: May 27, 2026

You can request deletion of Mali TikTok Studio data connected to your TikTok account at any time.

How to request deletion

  1. Email hello@malistudio.dev with the subject "Mali TikTok Studio Data Deletion".
  2. Include the TikTok username or email address used to connect the app.
  3. We will confirm the request, revoke stored access where applicable, and delete stored app data.

What will be deleted

  • Stored TikTok profile metadata used by the app.
  • Stored OAuth token records under our control.
  • Generated video metadata, captions, upload history, and scheduling records.

What may remain

Videos already published to TikTok remain controlled through TikTok. You can delete published TikTok videos from your TikTok account or app. Some security logs may be retained briefly where required for abuse prevention or legal compliance.
