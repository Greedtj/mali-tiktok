Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

Mali TikTok Studio helps the app owner create short 5-10 second vlog videos and publish approved videos to a connected TikTok account. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data we collect

  • TikTok basic profile information such as username, display name, avatar, and public profile details.
  • OAuth access tokens required to upload content after the user authorizes the app.
  • Selected media, generated video metadata, captions, scheduling details, and upload status.
  • Basic operational logs needed for security, troubleshooting, and preventing duplicate uploads.

How we use data

  • To identify the connected TikTok account in the dashboard.
  • To generate, schedule, and upload short videos selected by the account owner.
  • To show recent upload history and publish status.
  • To improve reliability and keep the automation secure.

Data sharing

We do not sell personal data. We do not share user data with advertising networks. Data is shared with TikTok only as required to publish videos through TikTok's official API.

Retention and deletion

We keep data only as long as needed to provide the automation workflow, comply with security needs, or respond to user requests. You can request deletion at any time by emailing hello@malistudio.dev.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Mali Studio at hello@malistudio.dev.
